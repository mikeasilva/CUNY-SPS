Welcome

This app was developed by Mike Silva as part of the CUNY SPSS DATA 608 course. This app has three parts to test proficiency with JavaScript. Part 1 have two apps: the text inverter and first twenty multiples. Part 2 pulls data from a CSV into a table. You can filter the table by searching a president by name. Part 3 integrates a toy API which makes a prediction of what Borough you are in based on the tree you see around you.
